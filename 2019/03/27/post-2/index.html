Work start on The Vault Escape

The Vault Escape / March 27, 2019 September 27, 2019

Work starts today for the new attraction ‘The Vault Escape’ at Hidden Valley Discovery Park.

This will be a reimagining of the much missed ‘Vault’ – one of HW Consulting’s first projects with Hidden Valley in 2005.

We can’t wait to welcome the first visitors to our largest attraction yet in summer 2021!
